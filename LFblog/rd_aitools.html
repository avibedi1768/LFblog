  1. 1. ChatGPT :

The crazy progress artificial intelligence (AI) has made lately has caused a stir in pretty much every industry you can think of. One AI superstar is ChatGPT, an AI chatbot that’s so cutting-edge, it’s practically doing linguistic backflips!

ChatGPT is an advanced AI language model developed by OpenAI. It represents a significant leap in natural language processing, enabling AI to generate coherent, contextually relevant, and almost human speech-like text responses in a conversational manner.

Now that you are acquainted, do not forget to ask this gem if your crush likes mango sorbet!

 

 

  1. 2. Beautiful AI:

 

It is stunning and beautiful what an AI model can do to your pitches, presentations and sorry not your pictures, you probably need a plastic surgery for them.

Now , about this beauty , “beautiful AI” ; Presentation software have design principles in place to help take on some of the burden of deck creation, and you should take advantage of that. Using a pre-built template can help you think of new ways to structure your presentation, while lending the ability to customize it where you see fit. As a good rule of thumb, less is more in presentations. Your design should be clean and professional, while keeping your company branding in mind. With new technology, many presentation platforms (excluding PowerPoint) will actually limit how much you can add to each slide to help avoid Frankendeck situations. Creating clear, concise slides are more impactful than cluttered ones. Make sure to stick to one key point on each slide so that your audience knows exactly what you want them to pay attention to.

  1. 3. Jasper AI:

When your teachers are empowered with tools that trap plagiarised write-up pieces, it is not a very good idea to copy and paste from the nooks and corners of internet and get yourself degraded. Getting caught has been a ritual for all of us brown kids but let us not do it here. But who wants to blabber 3000 random words on a pre-historic era case study?

To your rescue, we have the “Jasper AI”, Jasper is more than just a language model. Jasper’s AI Engine starts by pulling from a cross-section of the best models out there - including OpenAI's GPT-4, Anthropic and Google's models - then infuses those outputs with recent search data, your brand voice, and optimization tools like SEO and grammar. Because they have interoperability across models, they’re more reliable when things get choppy elsewhere. Smart!

 

  1. 4. Consensus:

This tool is excellent for conducting preliminary research and

compiling references. It’s essentially an AI-driven search engine that sources

its data from genuine research papers. Simply input a query and Consensus will

return relevant results, including the source, abstract, and a link to the

complete text. For certain sources, it also provides a quick tag, such as

indicating if it’s a rigorous journal or highly cited.

 

  1. 5. MeetGeek:

This tool is fantastic for any virtual lectures — MeetGeek automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes the content for you. It is compatible with Zoom and Teams. While this tool is primarily targeted towards professional use, it could prove beneficial for online students seeking an AI alternative for note-taking. A free plan is available, If your professors frequently use YouTube videos for teaching, this tool could be a time-saver. It’s a free Chrome extension that automatically transcribes and summarizes any YouTube video.
